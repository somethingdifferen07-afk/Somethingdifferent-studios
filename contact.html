Quick Answers

Frequently
Asked

How quickly can you start a project?

It depends on our current schedule. We typically onboard new projects within 1–2 weeks of signing. If you have an urgent deadline, mention it in your brief and we'll do our best to accommodate.

Do you work with clients outside India?

Absolutely. A significant portion of our work comes from international clients. We're comfortable working across time zones via video call, project management tools, and async review cycles.

What does your revision process look like?

Revision rounds depend on the project tier. Essentials projects include one round; Signature includes three; Bespoke engagements are unlimited within scope. All revisions are clearly defined in the contract.

Can I commission just a single service?

Yes. While many clients work with us across multiple services, we're happy to take on focused single-service projects — a standalone brand film, a motion sequence, or a graphic identity.

How is payment structured?

We typically work on a 50% deposit on project kick-off and 50% on final delivery. For larger retainer engagements we can discuss monthly billing arrangements.
